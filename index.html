About Us


If you are looking for something original, customized, and personal, then we can help. Every piece is unique and no two are the same. Everything is made from scratch, I keep no stock. From the CNC router to the laser engraver, resin art, Mod Podge, or a specific building material, anything is possible.


Quality costs. In our case it's mostly with time. Yes, the price may be higher than you can find elsewhere, but no one else will make it exactly the way you want it.


This is a part-time craft for me. I have a unpredictable, more than full-time job, plus family. If you need something in 3 days, I can't help. You can ask, but chances are not good unless my life schedule happens to be slow at that time. Depending on the project, turn-around can be from a week to a month or more. I also don't keep stock, so if parts and pieces need to be ordered, I'm also at that vendor's mercy.


Moral of the story, touch base early so we can get your project made!
